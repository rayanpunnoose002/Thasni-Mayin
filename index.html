About Us

Thasni Mayin Is A Successful Career Guide, Motivational Speaker And Life Coach. Her Way Of Presentation Is Highly Enthusiastic And Motivational Which Make People Realize And Recognise Their Potential And Also Assures Client Satisfaction In Every Respect. Her One-On-One Sessions With Students And Parents Makes Them Feel More Comfortable To Interact With.These Sessions Helps Students As Well As Parents To Get An Idea To Select The Right Career Path And Equip Students With The Capacity Of Decision Making In Life. She Provides Entertaining And Motivational Talk To Groups Also.

Thasni Started Her Career As Customer Relations Manager In Insurance Sector.With An Absolute Of 12 Yrs Of Experience In Customer Relation In Insurance, Telecommunication And Builders Sector, She Gained A Lot Of Achievments In The Field Of Client Relations.As A Career Coach, She Consider Customer Satisfaction As The Keystone Towards Success.From Insurance To Luxuary Villas, Interactions With Customers From Different Sectors Made Her A Fine Mentor.
